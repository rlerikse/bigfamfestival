# Big Fam 1.2.2 — Release Notes

## What's New
- **Live location sharing with friends** — see where your crew is on the map in real time.
- **Route to a friend** — tap a friend's marker to get directions straight to them.
- **Profile pics on the map** — you and your friends now show up as your avatars instead of generic pins.

## Improvements & Fixes
- **Schedule** now remembers your scroll position when you switch between list and grid views.
- Grid view heart icon cleaned up (centered, icon-only).
- Filtering the schedule by Stage/Genre no longer jumps you to the wrong spot.
- "Day over" now scrolls to the last event of the day, not the first.
- Home screen layout fixes: weather/clock sits correctly below the top bar, and the status bar no longer overlaps content on Android.
